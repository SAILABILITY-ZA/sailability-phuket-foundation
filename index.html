About Sailability Phuket Foundation

Sailability Phuket Foundation is a registered foundation that provides financial assistance to sailors with disabilities. Together with Disabled Sailing Thailand, we create opportunities for people of all abilities to experience the freedom of sailing, fostering independence, confidence, and inclusion while promoting adaptive sports in the region.

Our mission is to make sailing an accessible and enriching activity for individuals with disabilities, offering a unique opportunity to experience freedom and independence on the water. Based at the prestigious Royal Phuket Marina, our programs include tailored training sessions, regular sailing days, and participation in international and national competitive events, which collectively support physical wellness, boost mental health, and foster a strong sense of community among participants.

Our Fleet & Partnership

7 S\V14 Boats

Our fleet includes seven S\V14 boats specifically designed for accessible sailing - the world's premier adaptive sailing dinghies. Learn more about these innovative vessels at www.sv14.org

Gemini 505 Rescue RIB

Our professional Gemini Marine safety support vessel ensures the highest level of safety during all sailing activities, providing rescue and emergency response capabilities. Visit www.gemini-marine.com
